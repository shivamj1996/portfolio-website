Usability Evaluation

HCI/UX/Usability

In my Human Computer Interaction module I was given a task to conduct a usability evaluation on two similar websites. I chose Nike and Adidas as they are both e-commece type websites, and are both fashion companies.


I first researched about HCI principles, theories, and heuristics , and came up with a set of criteria that I would use whilst evaluating the websites. Then, I outlined the evaluation methods I would use, as well as participant information.

Following that, I gave an overall evaluation plan, detailing how I would record my results and gave a brief timeline of the evaluation day, as well as providing reasoning for my choice of methods.

I then presented my results and analysed them to find where usability in both websites could be improved, highlighting any significant errors found, as well as comparing both websites to each other. Finally, I produced a final analysis, as well as giving suggestions for improvements for both websites. Below is a download to the full report.
